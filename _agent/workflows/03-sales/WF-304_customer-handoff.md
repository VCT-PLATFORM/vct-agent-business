# WF-304: Customer Handoff

> Workflow: **AE → CSM → Support → Finance**

```
Deal Closed → Handoff Meeting → Onboarding Kick-off → Ongoing
    AE            AE+CSM              CSM              CSM+Support
```

## Flow
```
// turbo
1. AE prepares handoff brief (within 24h of close):
   ├── Customer context: Industry, size, use case
   ├── Champion + Decision maker contacts
   ├── Pain points discussed during sales
   ├── Promises made (features, timelines, SLAs)
   ├── Pricing/Contract details
   ├── Red flags or concerns
   └── Why they chose VCT (for retention playbook)

2. AE + CSM joint meeting (internal, 30 min):
   ├── Walk through handoff brief
   ├── Agree on onboarding approach
   ├── Set success milestones

3. AE introduces CSM to customer (warm intro email)
   ├── AE stays on CC for first 30 days
   └── Then fully transitions

4. CSM triggers onboarding: → SOP-501 (Customer Onboarding)

5. Finance: → SOP-303 (Invoice & Billing)
```
