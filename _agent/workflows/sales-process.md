# /sales-process — Quy trình Bán hàng

> **SOP-013** | Áp dụng khi: Tiếp cận khách hàng, follow-up, demo, proposal, hoặc chốt deal
> Actor: Sales Manager + Account Executive | Hỗ trợ: Marketing, Product, Legal

---

## Khi nào sử dụng
- Có lead mới cần tiếp cận
- Cần viết email outreach, proposal, script call
- Demo sản phẩm cho khách hàng
- Đàm phán và chốt hợp đồng
- Khi người dùng hỏi về sales, bán hàng, khách hàng

## Sales Stages

| Stage | Actions | Exit Criteria | Win % |
|-------|---------|-------------|-------|
| **1. Prospect** | Research, qualify ICP fit | ICP match confirmed | 10% |
| **2. Connect** | Outreach (email/call/social) | Positive response, meeting set | 15% |
| **3. Discover** | Discovery call (BANT+) | Pain confirmed, budget exists | 25% |
| **4. Demo** | Customized product demo | Champion engaged, "wow" moment | 40% |
| **5. Propose** | Send proposal/pricing | Proposal reviewed by decision maker | 60% |
| **6. Negotiate** | Terms discussion, legal review | Terms agreed | 80% |
| **7. Close** | Contract signed ✅ | Payment received | 100% |

## Quy trình Chi tiết

### Stage 1-2: PROSPECT & CONNECT
```
// turbo
Actor: Account Executive
├── Research: Company size, industry, tech stack, news, pain points
├── Personalize: 1 specific insight about THEIR business
├── Outreach sequence (xem AE SKILL.md):
│   ├── Day 1: Email 1 (Value-first)
│   ├── Day 3: LinkedIn connect
│   ├── Day 5: Email 2 (Case study)
│   ├── Day 7: Phone call
│   └── Day 10: Email 3 (Breakup)
├── Goal: Book a 30-min discovery call
```

### Stage 3: DISCOVER
```
// turbo
Actor: Account Executive
├── Discovery framework (BANT+):
│   ├── Budget: Confirmed or identifiable
│   ├── Authority: Decision maker identified
│   ├── Need: Pain points validated
│   ├── Timeline: When they need solution
│   ├── Champion: Internal advocate identified
│   └── Competition: Who else are they considering?
├── Outcome: Qualify IN or OUT (don't waste time on bad fits)
├── If qualified: Schedule demo
```

### Stage 4: DEMO
```
// turbo
Actor: Account Executive + Product Manager (for complex demos)
├── Rule: NEVER generic demo. Always customized to their pain
├── Structure (30-45 min):
│   ├── 5 min: Recap their pain points (prove you listened)
│   ├── 20 min: Show TOP 3 features that solve THEIR problems
│   ├── 5 min: ROI / Social proof (similar company results)
│   └── 5 min: Next steps + Timeline
├── After demo: Send summary email within 2 hours
```

### Stage 5-6: PROPOSE & NEGOTIATE
```
// turbo
Actor: Account Executive + Sales Manager
├── Proposal: Customized based on discovery and demo feedback
├── Pricing: Standard pricing ± approved discount range
│   ├── Sales Manager can approve up to 15% discount
│   ├── CMO can approve up to 25%
│   └── CEO for anything above 25%
├── Negotiation: Follow tactics in Contract Specialist SKILL.md
├── Legal: Contract Specialist drafts / reviews (trigger /contract-review)
```

### Stage 7: CLOSE
```
// turbo
Actor: Account Executive + Legal + Finance
├── Contract signed (digital signature preferred)
├── Finance: Invoice + Revenue recognition setup
├── Handoff to Customer Success Manager (warm intro)
├── Internal celebration 🎉
├── CRM updated: Deal closed, revenue logged
├── Lưu vào: marketing-sales/account-executive/memory/deliverables/
```
