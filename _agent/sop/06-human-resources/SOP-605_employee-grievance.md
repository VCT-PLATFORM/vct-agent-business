# SOP-605: Employee Grievance — Xử lý Khiếu nại Nhân sự

> **Actor**: CHRO + General Counsel | **Trigger**: Nhân viên khiếu nại hoặc report vấn đề

## Types of Grievance
| Type | Examples | Severity | Handler |
|------|---------|---------|---------|
| Work conditions | Workload, equipment, schedule | Low-Med | Manager + HR Ops |
| Interpersonal | Conflict with colleague/manager | Medium | CHRO |
| Harassment/Discrimination | Sexual harassment, bullying | High | CHRO + GC |
| Policy disagreement | Comp, benefits, promotion | Medium | CHRO |
| Whistleblowing | Fraud, ethics violation | Critical | CEO + GC |

## Process
```
// turbo
1. RECEIVE: Employee files complaint (verbal/written/anonymous)
   ├── Channel: Direct to CHRO, HR hotline, or anonymous form
   ├── All complaints must be acknowledged within 24h
   └── Document: Date, parties, nature of complaint

2. ASSESS: CHRO evaluates severity
   ├── Low: Mediate informally (CHRO + Manager)
   ├── Medium: Formal investigation (CHRO lead)
   ├── High: External investigation + Legal (GC involved)
   └── Critical: CEO + GC + External counsel

3. INVESTIGATE (if formal):
   ├── Interview complainant (detailed account)
   ├── Interview respondent (right to respond)
   ├── Interview witnesses (if any)
   ├── Review evidence (emails, messages, records)
   ├── Timeline: Complete within 15 business days
   └── Confidentiality: All parties instructed

4. RESOLVE:
   ├── Findings report (factual, no opinion)
   ├── Decision: Warning / Disciplinary / Termination / No action
   ├── Legal compliance: Follow BLLĐ 2019 disciplinary process
   │   ├── Written notice
   │   ├── Hearing with employee + union rep (if applicable)
   │   └── Document decision with signatures
   ├── Communicate to both parties
   └── Appeal: Employee has right to appeal within 7 days

5. FOLLOW-UP (30/60/90 days):
   ├── Monitor: No retaliation, no recurrence
   ├── Support: Counseling if needed
   └── Systemic: If pattern, update policies/training
```

## Zero-Tolerance Policy
- Sexual harassment → Immediate suspension pending investigation
- Fraud/Theft → Immediate suspension + Police report if criminal
- Violence → Immediate termination
