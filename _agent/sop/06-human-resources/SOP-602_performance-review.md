# SOP-602: Performance Review — Đánh giá Hiệu suất

> **Actor**: CHRO + Managers | **Frequency**: Quarterly formal + Weekly 1-on-1

## Process

### Weekly 1-on-1 (25 min)
```
// turbo
├── Manager-led but EMPLOYEE-owned agenda
├── Template:
│   ├── How are you feeling? (wellbeing check)
│   ├── Wins this week?
│   ├── Blockers / Challenges?
│   ├── How can I help?
│   ├── Development/Learning update
│   └── Anything else?
├── Document: Brief notes in manager's memory/discussions/
```

### Quarterly Performance Conversation (50 min)
```
// turbo
├── Self-assessment: Employee fills review form first
├── Manager assessment: Manager fills independently
├── Conversation: Compare, discuss gaps, align
├── Rating scale:
│   ├── 5: Exceptional — Consistently exceeds all expectations
│   ├── 4: Exceeds — Frequently exceeds expectations
│   ├── 3: Meets — Consistently meets expectations
│   ├── 2: Developing — Partially meets, needs improvement
│   └── 1: Below — Does not meet, PIP consideration
├── Dimensions:
│   ├── Results (50%): OKR/KPI achievement
│   ├── Competencies (30%): Skills, growth, learning
│   └── Values (20%): Culture, collaboration, ownership
├── Output: Agreed development plan (2-3 focus areas)
```

### Annual Calibration
```
// turbo
├── Managers present their team ratings to Dept Head + CHRO
├── Calibration: Ensure consistency across teams (same standard)
├── Forced distribution NOT required but reference:
│   ├── 10-15%: Rating 5 (Exceptional)
│   ├── 25-30%: Rating 4 (Exceeds)
│   ├── 40-50%: Rating 3 (Meets)
│   ├── 10-15%: Rating 2 (Developing)
│   └── 0-5%: Rating 1 (Below)
├── Compensation link: Rating → Salary increase + Bonus
├── Promotion consideration: 2+ quarters of Rating 4-5
```
