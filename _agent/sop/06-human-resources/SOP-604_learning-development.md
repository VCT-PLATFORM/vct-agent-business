# SOP-604: Learning & Development — Đào tạo & Phát triển

> **Actor**: L&D Specialist + Managers | **Trigger**: Skill gap, onboarding, career growth

## Training Types
| Type | When | Format | Duration |
|------|------|--------|---------|
| Onboarding | New hire | Structured program | 1-2 weeks |
| Skill-based | Gap identified | Workshop + Practice | 2-4 hours |
| Leadership | Promotion to manager | Coaching program | 3-6 months |
| Cross-functional | Career growth | Job shadowing / Rotation | 2-4 weeks |
| Self-directed | Ongoing | Budget + Time off | Ongoing |

## Individual Development Plan (IDP)
```
// turbo
├── Current state: Skills matrix (where am I now?)
├── Target state: Career goal (where do I want to be?)
├── Gap: What skills/experience do I need?
├── Plan: 70-20-10 model:
│   ├── 70% On-the-job: Stretch project, new responsibility
│   ├── 20% Social: Mentor, shadow a senior, join community
│   └── 10% Formal: Course, certification, book
├── Timeline: 6-12 months
├── Review: Monthly with manager during 1-on-1
```

## Annual Learning Budget
```
// turbo
├── Per person: $500-$2000/year (depending on level)
├── Covers: Online courses, Books, Conferences, Certifications
├── Approval: Manager for <$500, CHRO for >$500
├── ROI: Post-training: Share learnings with team (lunch & learn)
```
