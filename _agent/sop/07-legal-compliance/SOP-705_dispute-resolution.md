# SOP-705: Dispute Resolution — Giải quyết Tranh chấp

> **Actor**: General Counsel + CEO | **Trigger**: Tranh chấp thương mại, lao động, hoặc IP

## Dispute Escalation Ladder

```
Level 1: NEGOTIATION (Direct)
├── Timeline: 15 ngày
├── Action: Direct discussion between parties
├── Goal: Settlement agreement
└── Who: Account Manager/HR Ops (with GC guidance)

Level 2: MEDIATION (Third-party facilitated)
├── Timeline: 30 ngày
├── Action: Neutral mediator facilitates discussion
├── Cost: Low (VIAC mediation: ~5-15M VNĐ)
└── Who: General Counsel represents VCT

Level 3: ARBITRATION (Binding decision)
├── Timeline: 3-6 tháng
├── Action: Arbitrator(s) hear case, make binding decision
├── Cost: Medium (VIAC arbitration: ~20-50M VNĐ)
├── Preferred: For commercial disputes (faster than court)
└── Who: General Counsel + External counsel if needed

Level 4: LITIGATION (Court)
├── Timeline: 6-24 tháng
├── Action: File lawsuit at competent court
├── Cost: High (court fees + external lawyers)
├── Avoid if possible: Last resort
└── Who: External law firm + General Counsel supervise
```

## Pre-Dispute Checklist
```
// turbo
├── [ ] Document ALL communications (email, chat, call notes)
├── [ ] Preserve evidence (screenshots, contracts, invoices)
├── [ ] Review contract: Dispute resolution clause (what does it say?)
├── [ ] Calculate exposure: Financial + Reputational risk
├── [ ] Cost-benefit: Is it worth litigating? Or better to settle?
├── [ ] Insurance: Does our insurance cover this type of dispute?
├── [ ] Statute of limitations: How long do we have? (VN: typically 3 years)
└── [ ] CEO approval required before Level 3+ actions
```
