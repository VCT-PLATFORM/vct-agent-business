# SOP-207: Event Management — Tổ chức Sự kiện

> **Actor**: Marketing Manager + CMO | **Trigger**: Tổ chức webinar, hội thảo, meetup, hoặc tham gia event

## Event Types & Lead Time

| Type | Lead Time | Budget | Expected Leads |
|------|----------|--------|---------------|
| Webinar (online) | 2-3 tuần | Low | 50-200 |
| Workshop (online) | 3-4 tuần | Low-Med | 20-50 (high quality) |
| Meetup / Networking | 3-4 tuần | Medium | 30-100 |
| Conference (tham gia) | 6-8 tuần | High | 50-200 |
| Conference (tổ chức) | 12+ tuần | Very High | 200-1000 |
| Product Launch Event | 4-6 tuần | Medium | 100-500 |

## Quy trình (Webinar Example)

### T-3 tuần: PLAN
```
// turbo
├── Topic: Research trending topics, customer pain points
├── Speaker: Internal expert or external guest
├── Date/Time: Avoid Monday/Friday, 10-11 AM or 2-3 PM
├── Platform: Zoom / Google Meet / StreamYard
├── Landing page: Registration form + Agenda
```

### T-2 tuần: PROMOTE
```
// turbo
├── Email: 3 sends (announce, reminder, last chance)
├── Social: 5-7 posts across platforms
├── Personal invites: Sales team invite key prospects
├── Partner co-promotion (if applicable)
├── Target: 3x expected attendees (33% show rate)
```

### D-Day: EXECUTE
```
// turbo
├── Tech check: 30 min before (audio, video, slides, recording)
├── Moderator: Welcome, housekeeping, Q&A management
├── Record: ALWAYS record for replay value
├── Engagement: Polls, Q&A, chat interaction
├── CTA: Clear next step (demo, trial, download)
```

### Post-Event: FOLLOW UP
```
// turbo
├── Within 24h: Thank you email + Recording link + Resources
├── Within 48h: Sales follow-up on hot leads (attended + engaged)
├── Within 1 week: Content repurposing (blog, social clips, quotes)
├── Metrics: Registrations, Attendance rate, Engagement, Leads generated
├── ROI: Leads → Pipeline → Revenue attribution
```
