# SOP-204: Brand Management — Quản lý Thương hiệu

> **Actor**: Brand Manager + CMO | **Trigger**: Tạo/cập nhật brand assets, kiểm tra brand consistency

## Quy trình

### Brand Audit (Quarterly)
```
// turbo
├── Visual consistency: Logo, colors, fonts đúng chuẩn?
├── Voice consistency: Tone nhất quán across channels?
├── Competitive positioning: Differentiation vẫn clear?
├── Customer perception: Survey / NPS feedback
├── Digital presence: Website, social profiles updated?
└── Output: Brand Health Score + Action items
```

### Brand Asset Approval
```
// turbo
├── Mọi material công khai (ad, social, website, print) → Brand Manager review
├── Checklist:
│   ├── ✅ Logo usage đúng (clear space, min size, backgrounds)
│   ├── ✅ Color palette đúng (primary, secondary, accent)
│   ├── ✅ Typography đúng (headings, body, display)
│   ├── ✅ Imagery style consistent (photography, illustration)
│   ├── ✅ Tone of Voice đúng (professional yet approachable)
│   └── ✅ Legal disclaimers present (if needed)
├── PASS → Publish
└── FAIL → Feedback → Designer/Writer revise
```

### Brand Crisis
```
// turbo
├── Trigger: Brand bị dùng sai, trademark infringement, negative PR
├── Escalate: CMO → CEO → General Counsel (if legal)
├── Response: Follow SOP-106 Crisis Management
```
